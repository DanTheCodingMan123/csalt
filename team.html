Meet the Team

CSalt is organized into four subteams: Membrane, Mechanical, Community Connections, and Business. Our team of 51 students represents eight Cornell colleges: Engineering, Arts & Sciences, Human Ecology, CALS, AAP, ILR, Public Policy, and Dyson.
