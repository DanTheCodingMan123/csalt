Our Mission

At CSalt, our mission is to pioneer sustainable water solutions that harness the power of ocean wave energy. As a student-led initiative at Cornell University, we are dedicated to advancing eco-friendly desalination systems that not only meet global water demands but also uphold environmental stewardship. By collaborating closely with leading researchers and leveraging innovative engineering practices, we strive to create scalable desalination technologies that can be implemented worldwide. Through our efforts, we aim to contribute to a sustainable future, ensuring water security for communities around the globe while protecting our oceans and environment.

Our Values

Sustainability

CSalt is deeply committed to sustainability, focusing on innovative desalination technologies that minimize environmental impact while providing vital water resources. We strive for eco-friendly solutions that harmonize with nature, ensuring a sustainable future for all.

Innovation

At CSalt, innovation drives us to redefine the standards of desalination technology. We are dedicated to pioneering solutions that elevate the effectiveness of water purification while maintaining our commitment to the environment.

Community

CSalt values the power of community, embracing diverse perspectives and fostering global-mindedness. We prioritize collaborative efforts that not only advance our technological goals but also strengthen connections across cultures, aiming for a worldwide impact.
